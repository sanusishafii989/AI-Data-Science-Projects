## SLIDE 3 — THE SOLUTION

**Heading:**
CropGuard AI: Instant Diagnosis. Offline. On Any Phone.

**Three feature columns:**

Column 1 — icon: 📸
**Scan**
Take a photo of a crop leaf
with any smartphone camera.
No special equipment needed.

Column 2 — icon: 🤖
**Diagnose**
AI identifies the disease
in under 2 seconds.
Works without internet.

Column 3 — icon: 💊
**Treat**
Receive step-by-step
treatment advice in
plain language.

**How it works (below columns):**
MobileNetV2 (pretrained on 1.4M images) → Fine-tuned on PlantVillage
→ 15 disease classes across 5 crops → TFLite model runs on-device

**Key differentiators:**
✅ Fully offline inference — works in rural areas with no signal
✅ GPS logging — tracks disease spread across farms and villages
✅ Actionable advice — not just a label, but a treatment plan with costs
✅ Extensible — new crops can be added without rewriting the system

**Visual suggestion:**
Phone screen mockup showing the app: leaf photo on left,
"DIAGNOSIS: Tomato Late Blight 🔴 URGENT" card on right with
treatment steps below. Show confidence bar at 93%.

**Speaker notes:**
"CropGuard runs entirely on the phone — no internet required after the
first download. We chose MobileNetV2 because it was designed for
exactly this: accurate, lightweight inference on mobile hardware."
